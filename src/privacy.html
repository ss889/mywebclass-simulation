Privacy Policy

At MyWebClass.org, we respect the privacy of our users and are committed to protecting their personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose information about our users.

Information We Collect

We collect information from users when they register for an account, subscribe to our newsletter, or fill out a contact form on our website. The information we collect may include:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Mailing address
  • Phone number
  • Payment information (if applicable)
  • Other information as necessary to provide our services

How We Use Information

We use the information we collect from users to:

  • Provide and improve our services
  • Process payments (if applicable)
  • Send newsletters and other promotional materials
  • Respond to inquiries and customer support requests

Disclosure of Information

We may disclose information about our users in the following circumstances:

  • To our service providers who help us provide our services
  • To comply with applicable laws and regulations
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of MyWebClass.org, our users, or others

Cookies

We use cookies on our website to help us understand how users interact with our site and to improve their experience. Users can control the use of cookies through their browser settings.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the personal information we collect from our users against unauthorized access, use, and disclosure.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify users by email or by posting a notice on our website prior to the change becoming effective.

Google Analytics

At MyWebClass.org, we use Google Analytics to gather data about your website usage. This data includes how frequently you visit our website, which pages you access during each visit, and the sites you visited before coming to our website. We use this data to enhance our website and user experience. Google Analytics only collects your IP address on the date you visit our website, and this information cannot be used to identify you or obtain any other personal information. We don't combine data collected by Google Analytics with your personally identifiable data. Google Analytics installs a permanent cookie on your browser to recognize you as a unique visitor the next time you visit our website. This cookie is exclusively for Google's use and can't be accessed by anyone else. Google's access to and use of information gathered by Google Analytics concerning your visits to our website is limited by the Google Analytics Terms of Use and the Google Privacy Policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our practices, please contact us at privacy@mywebclass.org.
